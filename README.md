## What is Passr?

Passr connects students on campus, making it easy to find great deals on items they need or sell items they no longer use. Whether you're looking for a desk chair, selling your old laptop, or trying to find tickets to a concert, Passr helps you connect with other students in your community.

The app focuses on creating a trusted, localized marketplace where students can:
- Browse and search for items by category
- Post listings with photos, descriptions, and pricing
- Make offers and negotiate with sellers
- Chat directly with buyers and sellers
- Schedule pickup times and locations
- Manage their listings and track offers

## User Flows

### For Buyers
1. Browse or search for items on the home screen
2. View detailed product information and seller profile
3. Make an offer or add item to wishlist
4. Chat with seller to negotiate and ask questions
5. Schedule pickup time and location
6. Complete transaction

### For Sellers
1. Create a new listing with photos and details
2. Receive and review offers from interested buyers
3. Accept or reject offers
4. Chat with buyers to coordinate details
5. Schedule pickup times
6. Mark items as sold when transaction completes

## Technical Highlights

- **Cross-platform**: Built with React Native for iOS and Android

- **Real-time Features**: Chat and notifications system
